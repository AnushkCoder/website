JSMW Paryushan and Swapna Ceremony 2025

article/events·Aug 16, 2025·1 min read

In mid-August 2025, JSMW circulated reminders and logistics for the annual Paryushan observance and the associated Swapna Ceremony celebration. The messages framed the period as a countdown to the start of Paryushan and promoted participation in the community event.

Swapna Ceremony Announcement

On 2025-08-16, JSMW announced that only five days remained until Paryushan began and one week remained until the Grand Swapna Ceremony Celebration. The ceremony was scheduled for Sunday, 2025-08-24 at Laurel High School, beginning at 12:30 PM.

The announcement also highlighted two participation deadlines:

  • Dinner RSVP closed on Monday, 2025-08-18 at 5:30 PM
  • Fixed Nakro labhs were available through a dedicated signup sheet

The messaging emphasized urgency and encouraged members to secure labhs and RSVP promptly.

UMD Jain Kids Food Coordination

A related WhatsApp thread for UMD Jain Kids coordinated daily food for Jain students at UMD during Paryushan. The group shared a spreadsheet for signups and stated that food would be delivered on campus from 2025-08-20 through 2025-08-26, except on Sunday 2025-08-24 because of the Swapna Ceremony.

The thread also noted that Samvatsari would fall on 2025-08-27 and asked students to enter their names by the end of the day so arrangements could be made. The coordination was limited to students near the UMD campus.

Role

These messages show JSMW and related student groups using WhatsApp to coordinate religious observance, event attendance, fundraising-style labh signups, and campus meal logistics around Paryushan.
